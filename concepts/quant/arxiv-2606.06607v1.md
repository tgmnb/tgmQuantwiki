## 摘要

We investigate the LHC phenomenology of a vector dark-sector effective theory containing two neutral massive vector states, both odd under a dark-parity symmetry. The lightest state is stable and provides a dark-matter candidate, while the leading interactions with the Standard Model arise from dimension-six operators involving the hypercharge field strength. In the prompt-decay regime considered in this work, the heavier state can decay radiatively, leading to a $γ+\text{jets}+E_T^{\text{miss}}
