## 摘要

Numerical reservoir simulations are extremely computationally expensive, as they require the repeated solution of large nonlinear algebraic systems derived from the discretized governing equations. With growing demand for real-time optimization, uncertainty quantification, and history matching in digital twin applications, reducing computational cost has become essential. Deep learning (DL)--based surrogate models have emerged as an effective approach for accelerating subsurface flow simulations
