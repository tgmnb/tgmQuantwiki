## 摘要

Existing agentic reinforcement learning methods for GUI grounding have limitations at two levels. At the data level, current approaches typically treat all training samples equally, although their training value to the baseline model varies with difficulty. Overlooking this can greatly reduce training efficiency or even cause collapse.
