## 摘要

The surplus of a life insurance policy depends on both systematic changes in mortality risk and financial changes. We propose to decompose the surplus by the axiomatically justified IASU decomposition, which is a continuous time version of the Shapley value. However, life tables are not updated continuously, but rather, only once per year.
