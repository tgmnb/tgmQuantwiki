## 摘要

The financial market is a typical low signal-to-noise ratio (SNR) setting, which often destabilizes off-policy maximum-entropy methods like Soft Actor-Critic (SAC). Specifically, noisy state representations may produce unreliable Q-value estimates, and bootstrapping amplifies these errors, forming a failure mode we call the "Financial Entropy Trap". In this paper, we propose FPQC-SAC, an efficient and plug-and-play SAC variant that places a compact and bounded Parameterized Quantum Circuit (PQC)
