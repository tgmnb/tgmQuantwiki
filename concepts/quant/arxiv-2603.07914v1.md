## 摘要

We develop a new identification strategy for average treatment effects on the treated (ATT) in panel data with discrete outcomes. Standard difference-in-differences (DiD) relies on parallel trends, which is frequently violated in categorical settings due to mean reversion, out-of-bounds counterfactuals, and ill-defined trends for multi-category outcomes.
