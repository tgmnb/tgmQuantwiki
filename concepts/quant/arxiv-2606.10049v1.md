## 摘要

We study the dynamics of a heavy quark in a thermal plasma consisting of non-perturbatively interacting soft momentum gluons at high temperatures, described in terms of an effective theory of QCD discretized on a three-dimensional lattice. We propose a numerical strategy that allows us to simulate the dynamics of a heavy quark for different values of initial momenta in this thermalized plasma.
