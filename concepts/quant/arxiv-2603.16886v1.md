## 摘要

Multi-horizon price forecasting is central to portfolio allocation, risk management, and algorithmic trading, yet deep learning architectures have proliferated faster than rigorous financial benchmarks can evaluate them. This study provides a controlled comparison of nine architectures (Autoformer, DLinear, iTransformer, LSTM, ModernTCN, N-HiTS, PatchTST, TimesNet, and TimeXer) spanning Transformer, MLP, CNN, and RNN families across cryptocurrency, forex, and equity index markets at 4-hour and 2
