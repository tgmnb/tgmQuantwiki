## 摘要

We develop a branched signature kernel solver for linear and nonlinear ordinary differential equations driven by a \emph{single observed trajectory} of a possibly rough forcing signal -- a setting that arises naturally in earthquake engineering, finance, biology, and structural health monitoring, where the forcing is observed exactly once and the solver must respect the underlying physical law without recourse to an ensemble of realizations.
