## 摘要

In this paper we develop a stochastic heavy ball method for solving ill-posed inverse problems. The method updates the iterate using only a randomly selected equation at each iteration step while incorporating a momentum term into the process. To facilitate fast convergence, we propose an adaptive strategy for selecting the step size and the momentum coefficient.
