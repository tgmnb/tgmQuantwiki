## 摘要

Agentic reinforcement learning can induce tool abuse, where models overuse external tools even for queries solvable by internal reasoning. Existing approaches mitigate this issue with uniform tool-use penalties or hard limits, which reduce tool frequency but may also suppress useful tool-assisted exploration.
