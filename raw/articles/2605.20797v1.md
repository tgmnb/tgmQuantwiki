## Abstract

The present paper introduces a new representation-driven approach to per-instance algorithm selection, applied to black-box optimization, for automatically choosing the most promising solver from a fixed portfolio. Prior work in continuous optimization largely relies on numerical descriptors, including Exploratory Landscape Analysis features and learned embeddings such as Deep-ELA.
