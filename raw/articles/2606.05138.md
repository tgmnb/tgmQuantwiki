# Generating Financial Time Series by Matching Random Convolutional Features

**Authors:** Konrad J. Mueller, Nikita Zozoulenko, Ben Wood, Thomas Cass, Lukas Gonon

## Abstract

Generating realistic financial time series is challenging as training data is often limited to a single historical path. With such scarce data, overfitting is hard to avoid, especially under adversarial training where a trained discriminator can memorize the training samples. To mitigate this, recent approaches train generators to minimize the discrepancy between untrained feature representations of real and generated time series.
