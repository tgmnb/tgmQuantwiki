## Abstract

Electricity price forecasts are typically evaluated using accuracy measures such as RMSE and MAE, although these metrics often fail to reflect their economic value in operational decisions. This paper investigates which statistical properties of electricity price forecasts are most relevant for economic performance, using battery energy storage system (BESS) arbitrage as an application.
