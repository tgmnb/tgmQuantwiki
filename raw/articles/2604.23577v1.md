# RouteNLP: Closed-Loop LLM Routing with Conformal Cascading and Distillation Co-Optimization

**ArXiv ID:** [2604.23577v1](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.23577v1)

## Abstract

Serving diverse NLP workloads with large language models is costly: at one enterprise partner, inference costs exceeded $200K/month despite over 70% of queries being routine tasks well within the capability of smaller models. We present RouteNLP, a closed-loop framework that routes queries across a tiered model portfolio to minimize cost while satisfying per-task quality constraints. The framework integrates three components: a difficulty-aware router with shared task-conditioned representations
