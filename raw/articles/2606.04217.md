# Polymarket-v1 Database

**Categories:** Computational Engineering, Finance, and Science (cs.CE)

## Abstract

We introduce the Polymarket-v1 Database: the complete on-chain trade archive of Polymarket&#39;s first-generation CTF Exchange on Polygon, spanning 2022-11-21 to 2026-04-28 and covering the full contract lifecycle from first settlement to natural termination. The dataset comprises 1.20 billion trade records across 1.30 million markets with $61 billion in nominal volume.
