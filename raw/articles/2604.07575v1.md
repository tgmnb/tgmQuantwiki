# Robust Multi-Agent Target Tracking in Intermittent Communication Environments via Analytical Belief Merging

**ArXiv ID:** [2604.07575v1](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.07575v1)

## Abstract

Autonomous multi-agent target tracking in GPS-denied and communication-restricted environments (e.g., underwater exploration, subterranean search and rescue, and adversarial domains) forces agents to operate independently and only exchange information during brief reconnection windows. Because transmitting complete observation and trajectory histories is bandwidth-exhaustive, exchanging probabilistic belief maps serves as a highly efficient proxy that preserves the topology of agent knowledge.
