## Abstract

Dimensional accuracy in powder bed 3D printing of concrete is strongly influenced by binder distribution, and the resulting geometric deviations can be direction-dependent. This study examines how voxel-wise water dosage influences geometric fidelity and deviation anisotropy. Experiments show that small changes in water content can cause large, systematic deviations, including edge rounding and swelling.
