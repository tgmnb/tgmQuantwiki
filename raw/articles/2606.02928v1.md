## Abstract

Older adults are particularly susceptible to falls following perturbations during standing, such as forward loss of balance. Back support devices that assist trunk extension may help mitigate fall risk by preventing excessive trunk flexion. Previous studies have investigated heavy back support devices; however, these systems often introduced adverse effects on stability due to their added mass, which shifted the body's natural center of mass unfavorably.
