## Abstract

This paper examines the valuation and hedging of standard equity protection swap (EPS) products proposed by Xu et al.. To account for financial crises and counterparty default risk, we develop pricing frameworks based on Merton's jump-diffusion model and Szimayer's independent random time default model, under which closed-form valuation formulas and put-call parity relations for European options are derived.
