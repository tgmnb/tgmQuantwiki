## Abstract

Large Language Models (LLMs) are evolving into autonomous trading agents, yet existing benchmarks often overlook the interplay between architectural reasoning and strategy consistency. We propose Strat-LLM, a framework grounded in Stratified Strategy Alignment. Operating in a live-forward setting throughout 2025, it integrates heterogeneous data including sequential prices, real-time news, and annual reports to eliminate look-ahead bias.
