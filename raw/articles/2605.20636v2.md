## Abstract

This paper studies conditional allocation between a growth/technology ETF basket, denoted by $G$, and a defensive income/value-oriented ETF basket, denoted by $D$. The objective is not to discover a new standalone alpha factor, but to examine whether known style exposures can be dynamically allocated using macro-market timing signals.
