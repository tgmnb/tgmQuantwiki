## Abstract

Benchmarking forecasting architectures for daily equity portfolios is not just a prediction exercise. It also asks which model remains usable after preferences, costs, and portfolio constraints are imposed. We build a CRSP daily-stock benchmark for 15 deep and statistical time-series architectures over 2018--2024.
