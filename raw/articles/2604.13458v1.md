# Interpretable Systematic Risk around the Clock

**ArXiv ID:** [2604.13458v1](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.13458v1)

## Abstract

In this paper, I present the first comprehensive, around-the-clock analysis of systematic jump risk by combining high-frequency market data with contemporaneous news narratives identified as the underlying causes of market jumps. These narratives are retrieved and classified using a state-of-the-art open-source reasoning LLM. Decomposing market risk into interpretable jump categories reveals significant heterogeneity in risk premia, with macroeconomic news commanding the largest and most persist
