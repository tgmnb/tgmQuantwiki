## Abstract

In this work, we study the stochastic Schrödinger--KdV equation driven by additive noise from both analytical and numerical viewpoints. We first establish the evolution laws for the averaged plasmon number, momentum, and energy, together with the conservation of the averaged particle number. Motivated by these intrinsic structures, we develop two temporal discretizations.
