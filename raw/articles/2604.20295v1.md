# ETac: A Lightweight and Efficient Tactile Simulation Framework for Learning Dexterous Manipulation

**ArXiv ID:** [2604.20295v1](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.20295v1)

## Abstract

Tactile sensors are increasingly integrated into dexterous robotic manipulators to enhance contact perception. However, learning manipulation policies that rely on tactile sensing remains challenging, primarily due to the trade-off between fidelity and computational cost of soft-body simulations. To address this, we present ETac, a tactile simulation framework that models elastomeric soft-body interactions with both high fidelity and efficiency.
