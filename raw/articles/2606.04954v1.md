## Abstract

Hydrogenation is an effective strategy for enhancing electron--phonon coupling (EPC) and superconductivity in two-dimensional materials. However, excessively strong EPC can also induce lattice instabilities, leading to charge-density-wave (CDW) formation and structural phase transitions. Here, using first-principles calculations, we investigate CDW order in the Janus transition-metal chalcogenide hydrides 1T-WSH and 1T-WSeH.
