## Abstract

We conduct a systematic robustness analysis of the unsupervised machine learning module within the hybrid framework \texttt{USmorph}. This module automatically discovers morphological structures from large-scale galaxy images, forming the foundation of the complete classification workflow. We evaluate five pre-trained models for feature extraction and identify an ImageNet-pretrained AlexNet as the most effective for capturing discriminative morphological features.
