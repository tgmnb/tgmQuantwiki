# A Stochastic Thermodynamics Approach to Price Impact and Round-Trip Arbitrage: Theory and Empirical Implications

## Abstract

This paper develops a comprehensive theoretical framework that imports concepts from stochastic thermodynamics to model price impact and characterize the feasibility of round-trip arbitrage in financial markets. A trading cycle is treated as a non-equilibrium thermodynamic process, where price impact represents dissipative work and market noise plays the role of thermal fluctuations.
