## Abstract

In this paper we discuss an ongoing multi-year project that aims to make open text feedback more accessible and useful to UX practitioners by automating classification and providing real time access to comments, themes, and analysis. By significantly lowering the time and knowledge cost of implementing automated solutions, we aim to effectively democratize our data analysis processes, allowing and encouraging non-technical stakeholders to access and leverage data on their own.
