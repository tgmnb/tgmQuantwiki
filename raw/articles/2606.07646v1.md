## Abstract

Test-time adaptation (TTA) aims to align a model to shifting test domains using only unlabeled streaming data. Most existing methods implicitly infer a single global domain distribution, ignoring the multidimensional and sample-specific nature of real-world domain shifts, leading to fragile adaptation.
