# Capacity, Technology Portfolios, and the Paradox of Concentration

**ArXiv ID:** [2407.03504](https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.03504)
**PDF:** [https://arxiv.org/pdf/2407.03504](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2407.03504)
**Published:** 2026-06-04
**Categories:** General Economics (econ.GN)
**Authors:** Michele Fioretti, Junnan He, Jorge Tamayo

## Abstract

Does limiting the largest firm&#39;s capacity always lower prices? We model firms competing in supply schedules with multiple technologies, each defined by a constant marginal cost up to capacity. We show that capacity and technological efficiency coexist as distinct sources of market power, with opposite policy implications.
