## Abstract

AI is increasingly used to accelerate engineering design by improving decision-making and shortening iteration cycles. Application to marine propeller design, however, remains challenging due to scarce training data and the lack of widely available pretrained models. We address this gap with a physics-based data generation pipeline and a generative-AI framework for direct performance-to-design generation tailored to marine propellers.
