# FinStressTS: A Parametric Synthetic Benchmark for Time-Series Forecasting in Finance

**ArXiv ID:** [2606.03184](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.03184)

**Categories:** q-fin.CP, cs.LG, q-fin.ST
**Authors:** Jiaze Sun, Kelvin J.L. Koa, Ruiyang Ni

## Abstract

Financial forecasting is difficult due to low signal-to-noise ratios, latent factors, heavy tails, regime shifts, and jumps. Real-world benchmarks offer limited failure attribution: researchers can observe underperformance, but often cannot isolate why because mechanisms are unobservable and entangled.
