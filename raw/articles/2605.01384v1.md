## Abstract

Portfolio optimization is constrained by linear assumptions and insufficient integration of multi-modal information in traditional models. This paper proposes a cross-modal BERT-driven Actor-Critic framework SBCA for multi-asset portfolio optimization to address the deficiencies of existing deep reinforcement learning DRL methods in fusing price data and financial text sentiment, as well as lacking practical trading constraints.
