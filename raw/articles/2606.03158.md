# Portfolio Choice with Competing Precautionary and Accumulation Goals

## Abstract

We study optimal portfolio choice for a household simultaneously managing a random-deadline goal, such as a medical emergency or job loss, and a fixed-deadline goal such as retirement or college tuition. Under a forced funding rule, in which each goal is paid in full whenever affordable, the household maximizes a weighted sum of the probabilities of fully funding both goals in a Black--Scholes market.
