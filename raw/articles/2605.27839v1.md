## Abstract

This letter investigates a symbol-level precoder design for movable antenna (MA)-enhanced dual-functional radar-communication (DFRC) systems. To enhance radar sensing capabilities, we formulate an optimization problem aimed at maximizing the minimum radar signal-to-interference-plus-noise ratio (SINR) across multiple targets in a cluttered environment.
