# Markov Chain Decoders Overcome the Heavy-Tail Limitations of Lipschitz Generative Models

**ArXiv ID:** [2605.18931](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.18931)
**PDF:** [https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.18931](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.18931)
**Published:** 2026-06-04
**Categories:** Machine Learning (stat.ML)
**Authors:** Abdelhakim Ziani, Andras Horvath, Paolo Ballarini

## Abstract

Heavy-tailed distributions are prevalent in performance evaluation, network traffic, and risk modeling. This behavior poses a fundamental challenge for modern deep generative models.
