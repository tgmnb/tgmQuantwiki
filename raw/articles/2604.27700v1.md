## Abstract

The rapid growth of weather-dependent renewable generation increases price volatility and imbalance penalty risk in power markets, creating the need for advanced quantitative trading strategies. We develop a data-driven continuous-time stochastic optimal control framework for intraday electricity trading using stochastic differential equations with drift terms ensuring mean reversion to deterministic forecast trajectories.
