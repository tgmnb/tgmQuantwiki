## Abstract

Our work focuses on deep learning (DL) portfolio optimization, tackling challenges in long-only, multi-asset strategies across market cycles. We propose training models with limited regime data using pre-training techniques and leveraging transformer architectures for state variable inclusion. Evaluating our approach against traditional methods shows promising results, demonstrating our models' resilience in volatile markets.
