# Where Reasoning Breaks: Logic-Aware Path Selection by Controlling Logical Connectives in LLMs Reasoning Chains

**ArXiv ID:** [2604.20564v1](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.20564v1)
**PDF:** [https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.20564v1](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.20564v1)
**Published:** 2026-04-22
**Categories:** cs.CL, cs.CL
**Authors:** 

## Abstract

While LLMs demonstrate impressive reasoning capabilities, they remain fragile in multi-step logical deduction, where a single transition error can propagate through the entire reasoning chain, leading to unstable performance. In this work, we identify logical connectives as primary points of this structural fragility. Through empirical analysis, we show that connective tokens function as high entropy forking points, at which models frequently struggle to determine the correct logical direction.
