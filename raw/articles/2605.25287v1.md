## Abstract

Short carbon fiber-reinforced polymer (SCFRP) composites exploit the intrinsic conductivity of the carbon fiber network for self-sensing, yet no predictive model couples their anisotropic, rate-dependent fracture to piezoresistive damage identification. This work presents a finite deformation multiphysics phase-field framework coupling a viscoelastic-viscoplastic constitutive model, an anisotropic crack resistance formulation, and a piezoresistive conductivity model.
