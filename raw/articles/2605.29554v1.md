## Abstract

We derive the first-moment Shallow Water Exner Moment model with sediment entrainment and deposition (SWEMED1) and show that the full source term has a fully-settled water-at-rest equilibrium manifold. We prove that the model is only weakly hyperbolic at this equilibrium, which prevents the use of Yong's structural stability framework.
