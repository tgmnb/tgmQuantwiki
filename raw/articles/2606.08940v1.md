## Abstract

Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF) has significantly improved the quality and fluency of large language models in text summarization. However, its impact on affective properties remains insufficiently understood. In this work, we study sentiment drift, a systematic shift toward neutral sentiment in RLHF-based summarization outputs compared to source texts.
