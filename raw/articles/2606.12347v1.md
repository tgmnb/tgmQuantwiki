# Local Stress Redistribution Controls Interactions between Hydraulic Fractures and Pre-existing Fractures

**ArXiv ID:** [2606.12347v1](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.12347v1)

## Abstract

Hydraulic fracture (HF) propagation in naturally fractured formations is strongly influenced by local stress states near pre-existing natural fractures (NFs). The role of NF-induced shear deformation and stress redistribution in controlling HF trajectories remains poorly characterized.
