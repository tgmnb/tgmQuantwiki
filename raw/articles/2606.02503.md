# Pay Beliefs and the Amenity-Pay Tradeoff

**ArXiv ID:** [2606.02503](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.02503)
**PDF:** [https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.02503](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.02503)
**Published:** 2026-06-04
**Categories:** General Economics (econ.GN)
**Authors:** Martin Eckhoff Andresen, Manudeep Bhuller, Alfred Løvgren

## Abstract

This paper studies how workers&#39; beliefs about pay shape the tradeoffs between pay and workplace amenities. We design a multi-stage incentivized survey experiment that combines hypothetical choice experiments with elicited beliefs about starting salaries in real jobs and randomly varies the provision of explicit pay information.
