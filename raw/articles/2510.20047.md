# Pricing Variance Swap for Multi-Asset Stochastic Volatility Models

**ArXiv ID:** [2510.20047](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.20047)
**PDF:** [https://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.20047](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.20047)
**Published:** 2026-06-04
**Categories:** Mathematical Finance (q-fin.MF)
**Authors:** Semere Gebresilassie, Mulue Gebreslasie, Minglian Lin

## Abstract

This paper develops a novel framework for modeling variance swap of multi-asset stochastic volatility models by employing determinant-based instantaneous generalized variance. In this setting the determinant of the covariance matrix captures the joint dispersion of the multivariate log-return dynamics.
