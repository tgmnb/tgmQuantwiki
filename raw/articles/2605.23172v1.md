## Abstract

A computational/analytics framework for assessing the value of drill-hole information in ore grade estimation is described using Gaussian Process and statistics. A distinguishing feature is that it presents both a near-term and long-term vision, circumvents conditional simulations and avoids making rigid assumptions such as stationarity and uncorrelated errors.
