## Abstract

Numerical computation of stochastic control problems under partial observation is challenging because the dynamic programming formulation is naturally posed on the conditional distribution of the hidden state. We propose particle-based methods that replace this infinite-dimensional filtering state by a finite-dimensional weighted particle system, building on recent limit theory for mean-field control with common-noise-adapted controls.
