**ArXiv ID:** [2606.05882v1](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05882v1)

## Abstract

This paper examines the impact of market informedness on the profitability of market makers. In contrast to the existing literature, the analysis is conducted in a complex market environment featuring heterogeneous market-making agents that differ in terms of information sets and aversion to inventory risk, endogenous price formation, exogenous fundamental value dynamics, and self-exciting market order flow.
