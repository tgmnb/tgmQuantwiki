# Outbreak dynamics and population vulnerability in stochastic epidemic models on networks

## Abstract

During infectious disease epidemics, pathogen transmission occurs in host populations made up of interacting subpopulations. Using stochastic simulation and analytical approximations, we examine how outbreak sizes in networked populations depend on network architecture, subpopulation sizes and the strength of coupling between subpopulations.
