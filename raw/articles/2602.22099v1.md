## Abstract

Counterfeiting of premium spirits poses significant economic and health risks, that could be tackled by robust, accurate, portable and non-destructive through-bottle measurements. Here, we demonstrate the capability of focus-matched inverse spatially offset spectroscopy, combining fluorescence and Raman signals, for authenticating Cognac and Brandy.
