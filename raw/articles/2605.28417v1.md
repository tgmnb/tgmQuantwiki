## Abstract

This paper presents a unified multi-asset, multi-group asset-flow model that integrates three foundational frameworks from the behavioral finance literature. The model captures the dynamics of financial markets where multiple assets are traded by multiple investor groups, each with distinct trend-following (momentum) and value-based (fundamental) strategies.
