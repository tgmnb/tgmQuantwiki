## Abstract

Federated fine-tuning of Large Language Models faces severe statistical heterogeneity. However, existing model-level defenses often overlook the root cause: intrinsic data distribution mismatches. In this work, we first establish Federated Self-Distillation (FedSD) as a fundamental and potent strategy.
