## Abstract

Causal discovery from observational data underpins applications in finance, climate modeling, and machine learning. Constraint-based causal discovery reduces structure learning to a sequence of conditional independence (CI) tests, where each test decides independence by estimating conditional mutual information $I(X;Y \mid Z)$ to additive precision $τ$ and thresholding against it.
