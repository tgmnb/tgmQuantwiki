## Abstract

This study investigates the influence of fiber spatial distribution on the transverse mechanical properties of unidirectionally reinforced continuous-fiber composites. A Swelling & Random Migration algorithm was employed to generate representative volume elements with controlled fiber arrangements, ranging from clustered to equilibrium configurations.
