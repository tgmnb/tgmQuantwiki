# Recovering State Prices from Options

**ArXiv ID:** [2601.14852](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.14852)
**PDF:** [https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.14852](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.14852)
**Published:** 2026-06-04
**Categories:** General Finance (q-fin.GN)
**Authors:** Tjeerd De Vries

## Abstract

Extracting the joint risk-neutral distribution from option prices has remained an open problem since Ross (1976). We propose a projection-based estimator that approximates target payoffs over the entire state space using portfolios of observed options.
