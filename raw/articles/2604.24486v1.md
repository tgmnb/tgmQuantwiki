## Abstract

This study proposes a portfolio optimization framework that integrates advanced deep learning architectures with traditional financial models to enhance risk-adjusted performance. Using historical data from 2015-2023 across equities, ETFs, and bonds, the research evaluates the predictive power of Graph Neural Networks (GNNs), Deep Reinforcement Learning (DRL), Transformers, and Autoencoders.
