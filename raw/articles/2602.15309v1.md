# OSCAR: An Ovipositor-Inspired Self-Propelling Capsule Robot for Colonoscopy

**ArXiv ID:** [2602.15309v1](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.15309v1)
**PDF:** [https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.15309v1](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.15309v1)
**Published:** 2026-02-17

## Abstract

Self-propelling robotic capsules eliminate shaft looping of conventional colonoscopy, reducing patient discomfort. However, reliably moving within the slippery, viscoelastic environment of the colon remains a significant challenge. We present OSCAR, an ovipositor-inspired self-propelling capsule robot that translates the transport strategy of parasitic wasps into a propulsion mechanism for colonoscopy.
