## Abstract

Scene graph generation provides a compact structured representation for visual perception, but accurate and fast graph prediction from images and videos remains challenging. Recent VLM-based methods can generate scene graphs end-to-end as structured text, yet often produce long outputs with irrelevant objects and relations.
