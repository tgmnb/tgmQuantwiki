## Abstract

The International Council for Harmonization (ICH) E9 (R1) addendum provides the estimand framework to formulate treatment effects in a clinical trial. One of the attributes of an estimand the framework describes is intercurrent events. Among the five strategies to intercurrent events the guidance lists, the principal stratum strategy is the most conceptually and technically challenging because it defines treatment effects on unobserved strata.
