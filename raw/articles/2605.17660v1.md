## Abstract

Transformers have become the dominant architecture in modern machine learning, yet the theoretical understanding of their training dynamics remains limited. This paper develops a rigorous mathematical framework for analyzing gradient-based training of transformers in the mean-field regime, where both the depth (number of layers) and width (number of attention heads) tend to infinity.
