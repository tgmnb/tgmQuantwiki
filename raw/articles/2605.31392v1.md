## Abstract

Polyconvex constitutive modeling is attractive as it guarantees stability of numerical simulations and can improve the generalization behavior of material models. However, in certain applications, polyconvex formulations perform poorly in reproducing the underlying ground truth material response, which can effectively preclude their practical use.
