## Abstract

Anomaly detection methods in financial time series score statistically unusual observations in observable data, not topologically misexpected persistent deviations in the latent structure of co-movement. This study constructs a stock-level topological anomaly score jointly conditioned on market-level topological structure and cross-sectional peer context, and tests whether its history carries predictive content for return curves.
