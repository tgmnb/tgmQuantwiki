## Abstract

Parametric insurance contracts translate index measurements to compensation for policyholders' losses using predefined payment schemes. These need to be designed carefully to keep basis risk, i.e. the disparity between payouts and true damages, small. Previous research has motivated the use of conditional expectiles as payment schemes, whose compensation is impacted by the policyholder's potentially unknown attitude towards basis risk.
